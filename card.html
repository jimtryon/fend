Lion

A lion can run for short distances at 50 mph and leap as far as 36 feet.

  • Scientific Name: Felidae
  • Average Length: 182 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Savannahs and grasslands

Yellowish red and dark brown with dark spots, the lion is one of the largest mammals in the Africa. They are known for their prominent mane and have been widely recognized in paintings, films, and literature.
